We bring data to life.

Human Environmental Attunement through Real Time Sensing

HEARTS transforms invisible environmental forces into tangible experiences. Through real-time sonification and visualization of scientific data, we reveal imperceptible phenomena—solar storms, volcanic tremors—fostering deeper connection and understanding of the natural systems that shape our world.

HEARTS debuted the "Volcano Ride" Experience at UCLA's Exploring Your Universe 2025 symposium. Attendees experienced the seismic vibrations of five active volcanoes in real time.
